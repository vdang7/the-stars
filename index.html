Reserve by phone

Reservations are an arrangement guests make in advance, confirming a table for them at the specified time. Reservations are made by phone, through our restaurant’s website, third-party reservation sites or apps, at the restaurant in person, or even with a text message. Convenience is key when it comes to diners getting a seat at our venue.

Private dining & events

Whether you’re planning a business meeting or cocktail reception, we are your home for private dining events. We offer two private dining rooms with customized menu options and complimentary wireless internet. A food & beverage minimum and completed contract is required to book our private event rooms.
